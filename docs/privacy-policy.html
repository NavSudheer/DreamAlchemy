🔮 Dream Alchemy - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

🔮 Introduction

Dream Alchemy ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Dream Alchemy mobile application (the "App").

📱 Information We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • Dream Descriptions: Text you enter for analysis
  • Account Information: Email address (if provided)
  • Subscription Data: Purchase information through App Store

Automatically Collected:

  • Usage Analytics: App features used, session duration
  • Device Information: Device type, OS version, app version
  • Trial Tracking: Analysis count, trial start date (stored locally)

🛡️ How We Use Your Information

Primary Uses:

  • Dream Analysis: Process your dream text through AI services
  • Subscription Management: Track trial usage and subscription status
  • App Improvement: Analytics to enhance user experience
  • Customer Support: Respond to inquiries and technical issues

AI Processing:

  • Dream text is sent to secure AI services for analysis
  • Analysis results are returned and stored locally on your device
  • We do not train AI models on your personal dream content

💳 Subscription & Payment Information

Trial System:

  • Local Tracking: Trial usage (3 analyses + 7 days) stored on your device
  • No Payment Required: Trial does not require payment information
  • Fair Usage: Trial limits prevent abuse while protecting user privacy

Subscription Processing:

  • Apple App Store: All subscription billing handled by Apple
  • RevenueCat: We use RevenueCat for subscription management
  • No Card Storage: We never store payment card information
  • Subscription Data: Purchase status and subscription tier only

🔒 Data Security

Encryption:

  • All data transmission uses industry-standard SSL encryption
  • Local data stored securely on your device
  • No dream content stored on our servers permanently

Third-Party Services:

  • AI Analysis: Secure, encrypted transmission for dream analysis
  • RevenueCat: GDPR and CCPA compliant subscription management
  • Apple Services: App Store policies apply for payment processing

📊 Data Sharing

We DO NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share dream content with advertisers
  • Use your dreams for marketing purposes
  • Store dream text permanently on our servers

Limited Sharing:

  • AI Services: Dream text sent for analysis only (not stored)
  • Analytics: Anonymized usage data for app improvement
  • Legal Requirements: Only when required by law

👤 Your Rights

Access & Control:

  • View Data: See what data we have about you
  • Delete Account: Request deletion of your account data
  • Correct Information: Update or correct your information
  • Export Data: Request a copy of your data

Subscription Rights:

  • Cancel Anytime: Manage subscriptions through App Store
  • Refund Policy: Apple's refund policy applies
  • Restore Purchases: Re-download subscription on new devices

🧒 Children's Privacy

Dream Alchemy is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

🔮 Dream Analysis Specifics

How Dream Analysis Works:

  1. You enter dream text in the app
  2. Text is sent securely to AI analysis service
  3. Analysis results returned and displayed
  4. No permanent storage of dream content on our servers
  5. Results stored locally on your device only

Your Dream Privacy:

  • Temporary Processing: Dreams processed but not permanently stored
  • Local Storage: Full analysis stored only on your device
  • No Human Review: Dreams are processed by AI only, not read by humans
  • No Profiling: We don't create personality profiles from your dreams

For privacy questions or requests:

  • Email: support@dreamalchemy.app
  • Subject Line: "Privacy Policy Inquiry"
  • Response Time: Within 48 hours

⚖️ Legal Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
  • Apple App Store privacy requirements
  • COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)
